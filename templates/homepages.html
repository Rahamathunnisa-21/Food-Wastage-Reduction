Reduce food waste

Food choices affect your health and the environment
.Between 30% and 40% of food in the india is thrown out or
wasted each year,according to the Department of Agriculture.
That equals about 133 billion pounds and $161 billion.
Food loss occurs at every step in the food supply chain
from production to consumption Some examples are spoilage during
storage and transport,or exposure to insects and rodents,
bacteria or mold. Sorting out blemished or imperfect
produce results in loss,as does consumers buying more than
they need or can consume Not only is edible food wasted, but all
the energy,fertilizer and land use that went into to producing
that food is wasted as well.
